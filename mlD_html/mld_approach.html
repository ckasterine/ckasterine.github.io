A life long practitioner of martial arts and Chinese medicine

Marshall's expereince with Chinese medicine has inspired a deep understanding of health maintenance through the use of Qi Gong exercise. He has studied Kung Fu, Kali Arnis, and Ba Gua. Marshall also apprenticed with Tom Bisio, a renowned Zheng Gu Tui Na teacher.

Since childhood, Marshall has practiced and studied external martial art forms. In his 30s Marshall began focusing on the internal styles of MA. Following the Asian standard utilizing Yin and Yang ( balancing of external energies with the internal energies) philosophy.

When he began formal training in Chinese Medicine and Bodywork he realized the importance of the underlying structural alignment necessary to properly utilize the healing techniques of Tui Na massage. This alignment is common to Body work and MA disciplines.

Marshall is a graduate of Tri-State College of Acupuncture (2002), where he will be conducting Grand Rounds at TSEA in 2017 and is currently developing a Tui Na/Bodywork curriculum in the new year.
