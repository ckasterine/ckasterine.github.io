An Integrative Approach to Healing

Marshall's understanding of Chinese medicine and health maintenance has been greatly informed by the fundamental exercises and body mechanics found in martial arts. A New York State licensed practitioner and an NCCAOM certified member, he has been treating patients for over 15 years. He has studied Kung Fu, Aikido, Kali Arnis, and Ba Gua. Marshall also spent 6 years apprenticing with Tom Bisio, a renowned Zheng Gu Tui Na teacher and martial artist.

Since childhood, Marshall has practiced and studied martial arts. In his late 20s, he began focusing on the internal styles of martial arts following the Asian doctrine of balancing Yin and Yang. Through his years of study and practice of Chinese medicine and martial arts, Marshall realized that the underlying structural alignments are important and necessary to properly utilize the healing techniques of Acupuncture and Tui Na.

Marshall is a graduate of Tri-State College of Acupuncture (2002), where he is currently conducting Grand Rounds. In addition, Marshall is developing a Tui Na Bodywork curriculum that can be applied towards fulfilling continuing education unit (CEU's) requirements.
