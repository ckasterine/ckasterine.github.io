All Bodies Yoga Pilates studio has moved to 1135 Route 9D in Garrison, NY.
The new studio is a sunny yellow cottage, conveniently located between the
Garrison post office and Garrison Cafe.

FROM THE NORTH:
Traveling south on 9D, All Bodies Yoga Pilates is on the right (on the river side).
The entrance to the parking lot is shared with the Garrison Cafe, just after the Garrison Post office.

FROM THE SOUTH:
Traveling north on 9D, go through the traffic light in front of the Garrison Union Free School (on your right). Immediately to your left, you will see the Garrison Cafe which shares a parking lot with All Bodies Yoga Pilates.
